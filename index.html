About El Curichal Hostel

Located in the heart of Rurrenabaque, Bolivia, El Curichal Hostel offers comfortable accommodations for adventurous travelers seeking to explore the Amazon rainforest and the stunning pampas region. Our hostel combines authentic Bolivian hospitality with modern amenities to ensure your stay is both memorable and comfortable.

Whether you're here for jungle expeditions, wildlife watching, or cultural experiences, our friendly staff will help you make the most of your Bolivian adventure.

Prime Location

Rurrenabaque, Bolivia

El Curichal Hostel is strategically located in the heart of Rurrenabaque, the gateway to both the Amazon rainforest and the pampas region. Our location offers easy access to:

  • Rurrenabaque Airport (5 minutes walk)
  • Local markets and shops (2 minutes walk)
  • Restaurants and cafes (1 minute walk)
  • River port for boat tours (3 minutes walk)
  • Bus terminal (10 minutes walk)
