About El Curichal Hostel

El Curichal Hostel features an outdoor swimming pool, garden, a shared lounge and bar in Rurrenabaque. Conveniently situated in the heart of Rurrenabaque, this property puts you close to attractions and interesting dining options, making it the perfect base for your Bolivian adventure.

Featuring family rooms and providing guests with a sun terrace, we offer a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, concierge service and free WiFi. Whether you're here for jungle expeditions, pampas tours, or cultural experiences, our helpful multilingual staff will ensure your stay is memorable.
